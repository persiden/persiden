  • TO AVOID HAVING CLASSES DROPPED FOR NONPAYMENT, you must remit payment in full by August 3, 2015 for Fall Semester and December 15, 2015 for Spring Semester.
  • Accepted payment methods are cash, checks, money orders, web credit card and web checking account payments.
  • Payment by debit or credit card (MasterCard, VISA, American Express, and Discover) is available online and will result in an additional charge for a 2.75% convenience fee.
  • Online payment from a checking account is also available for current students at no charge.
  • To pay by credit or debit card or web check go to wcu.edu, and then choose one of the following:

CURRENT STUDENTS
  1. Select My Cat and log in.
  2. Select Personal Services and then Student tab.
  3. Select Account and Payment Information.
  4. Select Bill + Pay.
  5. Enter the Bill + Payment Account Suite.
  6. Click on Make a Payment .
  7. Click Current account balance or enter an amount in box and click Continue.
  8. Select the payment method and enter the credit card or bank routing information.

PARENT/GUESTS
  1. Select Parents.
  2. Choose the Making a Tuition Payment link under Payments & Finances.
  3. Click on the Authorized User Login link
  4. Enter the authorized user information.
  5. On the right, under action, click on Pay.
  6. Select the payment method and enter the credit card or bank routing information.

TO SETUP AN AUTHORIZED USER
  1. Go to wcu.edu and click on MyCat.
  2. Enter your Student ID and Password.
  3. Click on Personal Services and then Student tab
  4. Select Account and Payment Information.
  5. Click Bill + Payment
  6. Click the Authorized Users tab.
  7. Provide the email address and click Continue
  8. Authorize the user by clicking I Agree and click Continue.
The authorized user will receive an email with a link to the website and a password to access your account. The user will be prompted to change the password the first time the account is accessed.

TO SETUP DIRECT DEPOSIT REFUNDS
  1. Go to wcu.edu and click MyCat.
  2. Enter your Student ID and Password.
  3. Click Personal Services and then Students tab.
  4. Select Account and Payment Information.
  5. Click Bill + Payment
  6. Select the eRefund tab and then Set Up Account.
  7. Provide bank account information, confirm account and routing numbers, and click Continue.
  8. Check the I Agree box and click Continue.
