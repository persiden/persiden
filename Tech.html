Technology Commons

Facebook | YouTube | Twitter


The Technology Commons, part of the Division of Information Technology (DoIT), is designed as the single walk-up location for students, faculty, and staff of WCU to access IT resources. The area includes spacious computer workstations, relaxed laptop friendly study areas, and technology enhanced collaborative spaces. The Technology Commonsâ€™ services are free with the exception of non-warranty hardware repair and printing. Free services include general IT assistance, software repair, Apple & Dell warranty repair, software training, Catamount Mail support, equipment for checkout, in-room IT assistance, and residential network support. The area also has large screen displays, scanners, and specialty software (i.e. Adobe Master Collection, iLife, Final Cut, and more!). The result is a unique, comfortable location where students can enrich their total academic experience while having IT resources at their disposal.

The DoIT provides free downloads of Microsoft Office and antivirus to all WCU students, faculty, and staff.

Location: ground floor Hunter Library
