Campus Dining Services

WCU Campus Dining

Dining Services

WCU’s meal plans are comprised of three things: Declining Balance, Block Meals and Meal Exchange

Declining Balance Points

Declining Balance Points work on the same principle as bank debit cards. Each time your student makes a food purchase, the total cost of the meal is subtracted from the declining balance points in their account. Declining balance points may be used at any campus dining location.
Unused declining balance points will transfer at the end of fall semester to the spring semester. However, any declining balance points remaining at the end of spring semester are forfeited to the University.

Block Meals

Block meals are prepaid meals that can be used in the all-you-care-to-eat (AYCE) Courtyard Dining Hall. Block meals are great because they oﬀer a discounted rate for buying upfront rather than paying the door price.
The amount of meals on a meal plan is by semester and does not carry any weekly restrictions. Students may use their block meals for friends and family with any plan other than the unlimited plans. Block meals expire at the end of each semester and will not carry over from fall to spring.

Meal Exchange

The meal exchange program will begin fall 2015. Students will have the opportunity to use a portion of their block meals at certain retail locations. There will be a list of meals that students can choose from at each participating location.
Unused exchange meals do not carry over from the fall semester to the spring semester.

Meal Plans

For information regarding our Fall 2015 and Spring 2016 Meal Plans please refer to our website: www.westerncarolina.campusdish.com As of January 1, 2014 a new NC state law requires meal plans and food purchases to be taxed 6.75%. If paying with CatCash, cash or credit at the register, tax will be added on at the time of purchase.

Locations

Students at WCU are able to enjoy a variety of choices when it comes to dining on campus. WCU Dining has eleven retail locations on campus including top brands such as McAlister’s Deli, Which Wich, Starbucks, Chick-Fil-A, Moe’s Southwest Grill, Panda Express and Papa John’s. Also, placed throughout campus, there are three C-Stores available to grab quick convenience items. Students also have the opportunity to eat in our all-you-care-to-eat dining hall located on the second ﬂoor of the Courtyard Building.
