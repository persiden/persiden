Advising Center

214 Killian Annex
828-227-7753
advisingcenter@wcu.edu
advising.wcu.edu


When you visit the Advising Center, you will find a staff of professional advisors who are ready to:

  • help you select appropriate courses and discover other educational experiences that will help you meet your goals
  • answer your questions about university policies and our educational programs
  • talk to you about our educational resources that can help you make the most of your college experience
  • share with you information about support services that are available to help meet your specific needs
  • assist you in establishing goals and help you track your progress towards those goals
  • help you explore areas of study and corresponding career options
  • actively listen to your questions and concerns
During the first few weeks of your first semester, you will meet with your professional advisor to get better acquainted and learn more about campus resources that will help you make the most of your college experience and to answer any questions you may have.

Located in the center of campus in the OneStop building, the staff of the Advising Center is available to assist you throughout the year.
