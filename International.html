International Programs and Services

ips.wcu.edu
iep.wcu.edu


Study Abroad

  • Study abroad in a classroom setting or as an international internship/service learning
  • Receive academic credit in major, minor, or liberal studies
  • Pay only WCU tuition and fees in many instances
  • Study abroad for up to three semesters (as many summers as you wish)
  • Near 40 overseas higher education partners

International Students

  • 170 International Students representing 23 countries


Major International Activities

  • International Education Week in Fall semester, a combination of academic lectures and programs that center on international culture
  • International Festival in Spring semester, celebrating the diversity of WCU campus.

For more information, http://ips.wcu.edu , international@wcu.edu , TEL: (828) 227-7494

Intensive English Program (IEP) at WCU

  • Quality English language instruction for non-native speakers
  • Preparation for academic study in the United States
  • Four levels of English instruction
  • Instruction and strategies in academic reading, writing, listening and speaking
  • 18+ hours per week in language instruction and activities


IEP Highlights

  • Small class size
  • Orientation to the program and WCU
  • Access to all facilities of WCU
  • Opportunities to learn about American culture
  • Extracurricular activities
  • Service Learning opportunities

For more information: http://iep.wcu.edu

Contact: Jill Cargile Director of the Intensive English Program jscargile@wcu.edu 828-227-3458
