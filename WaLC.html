Writing and Learning Commons

WaLC TUTORING



Our Services…

  • Trained graduate and undergraduate peer tutors
  • One-on-one writing tutoring
  • Small-group course tutoring
  • Drop-in tutoring for all lower division math classes and math concepts in other disciplines
  • Individual academic skills consultations
  • Online, open-access writing, research, and learning resources
  • Writing, research, and academic skills workshops
  • Writing Fellows: a curriculum-based peer tutoring program that places trained undergraduate tutors in eligible courses
  • Documentation of students’ tutoring visits, communicated to course instructors weekly

To schedule…

Course tutoring appointments, writing appointments, and academic skills consultations: Make an appointment from the WaLC website or call 828-227-2274. Drop-in writing tutoring also available on a first-come, first-served basis. Mathematics tutoring sessions Visit the Math Tutoring Center website for hours of operation. Tutoring is available on a drop-in basis—no appointment necessary!
